Scene Description:
A vast underwater research station, a beacon of light and technology in the deep. Multiple interconnected domes house laboratories, greenhouses, and observation decks. Outside the transparent hull, schools of glowing fish swirl gracefully, and distant shadows hint at massive creatures in the abyss. Inside, scientists coordinate quietly at their consoles, holographic marine data rotating above the central table. Blue-white ambient light fills the space, shifting subtly with the movement of the ocean currents.

Camera and Motion:
The camera starts outside the base, moving slowly through the dense water toward the glowing habitat dome. As it passes through the hull, the lighting transitions from cold deep-blue to warm white interior hues. A sweeping crane shot moves through the main atrium, past floating workstations and curved hallways, before centering on a marine biologist analyzing holographic data. The sequence ends with a slow orbit around the base, showcasing the structure illuminated like a jewel in the dark ocean.
